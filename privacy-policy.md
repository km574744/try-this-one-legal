# Privacy Policy — Try This One

*Last updated: March 2026*

Try This One ("the App") is an AI-powered tool for UGC creators. This policy explains what data the App collects, where it goes, and how long it's kept.

---

## What We Collect

**Text you enter.** When you use the Brief & Ideation feature, the product name, description, and details you type are sent to our proxy server and forwarded to the Anthropic API to generate creative angles and video ideas. This text is not stored by us after the response is returned.

**App preferences.** Settings you choose (feedback tone, experience level) are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

**Saved ideas.** Ideas you save are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to them.

---

## Where Your Data Goes

Requests from the App are routed through a proxy server hosted on Railway, which then calls:

- **Anthropic API** — to generate creative angles and video ideas from your text input
- **Google Sheets** (read-only) — to serve the curated audio library in the Market Research tab

No data is sold, shared with advertisers, or used to train AI models.

---

## Data Retention

- **Text inputs** are not retained by us after a response is returned. Anthropic's data handling is governed by [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).
- **Local storage** (preferences and saved ideas) lives on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
