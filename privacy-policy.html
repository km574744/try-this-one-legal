Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Try This One is an AI-powered creative tool for UGC creators. This policy explains what data the App collects, where it goes, and how long it's kept. The short version: we collect as little as possible, store almost nothing on our end, and don't sell or share your data.

What We Collect

Brief text

When you use the Ideas feature, the product name and details you type are sent to our proxy server and forwarded to the Anthropic API to generate creative angles and captions. This text is not stored by us after the response is returned.

App preferences

Your name and settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Saved ideas & notes

Ideas you save and sticky notes on your home screen are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to them.

Where Your Data Goes

Requests from the App are routed through a proxy server hosted on Railway, which calls:

Anthropic API

Generates creative angles and captions from your brief text. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Google Sheets (read-only)

Serves the curated audio library in the Market Research tab. No user data is sent to Google Sheets.

No data is sold, shared with advertisers, or used to train AI models.

Data Retention

Brief text inputs are not retained by us after a response is returned.

Local storage — preferences, saved ideas, and home screen notes — lives on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
